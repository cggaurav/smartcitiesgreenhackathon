Awair


Real-time & cheap sensor fusion of air, noise and light pollution to provide actionable insights to make outdoor and indoor environments liveable and green.


Why

Throughout the world, including both developing and developed countries, air, noise & light pollution has a negative impact on health and environment. However, there is still a lack of scientific data and this impacts on expanding public understanding and awareness of this issue.

How

  • Air - measure smoke, CO2, alcohol, dust and allergic compounds
  • Sound - measure and analyze sounds from rail, air, construction etc
  • Temperature and Humidity
  • Light - what if it was too bright in the evening or too dark in the morning?
